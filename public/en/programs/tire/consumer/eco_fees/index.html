Recycling Fee

Consumers pay a recycling fee( Eco Fee) to the retailer on each new tirethey buy. The retailer submits the fees collected to Recycle NB. Then Recycle NB uses those funds to help pay for the tire-recycling program. The program is self-supporting and receives no money from government.

Tire-recycling Fees Paid by Consumers

Motorcycle & Moped Tire

$3.00

Passenger & Light Truck Tire

$4.50

Medium Truck Tire

$13.50

 

 

 

 

  

For more details on each fee category please see our FAQ.

Off-the-road (OTR) tires currently are excluded from the Designated Materials Regulation
